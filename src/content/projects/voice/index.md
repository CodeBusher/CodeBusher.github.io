V.O.I.C.E. is an AI-powered virtual-patient simulation platform for speech-language pathology (SLP) education. Students practice clinical encounters with simulated patients across realistic scenarios — building interview, assessment, and counseling skills before they meet real patients.

I lead the project under the supervision of Prof. Yoon, working across a Unity-based client and the live web platform.
